About Us

25 years of craft.
Every job, done right.

We're a hands-on lighting repair shop serving all five boroughs of New York City — built on experience, precision, and a genuine love for the craft.

Our story

More than a repair shop.

With over 25 years in the trade and UL certification behind every job, we've built a reputation for taking on the pieces other shops turn away — antique floor lamps, ornate chandeliers, vintage sconces that haven't been touched in decades.

This isn't a high-volume operation. Every fixture that comes through our door gets the full attention of our head technician — assessed by hand, repaired with care, and returned to you working exactly as it should.

We believe a well-made lamp deserves a well-made repair. No shortcuts, no guesswork — just the kind of work that lasts.
