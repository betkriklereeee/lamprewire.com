Common questions

Good to know
before you visit.

Do you offer pickup or delivery?

We are a drop-off service only. Please bring your fixture directly to our shop — we do not offer pickup or delivery at this time.

How do I get started?

Give us a call to schedule your drop-off. We'll find a time that works and make sure we're ready for your piece when you arrive.

How long will my repair take?

Most repairs have a 2-day estimated turnaround. After your fixture is assessed, our head technician will call you personally to discuss the repair and timeline.

Is there a fee for an estimate?

Estimates are completely free and come with no commitment. We'll assess your fixture and give you a full breakdown before any work begins.
