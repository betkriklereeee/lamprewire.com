Chandelier Repair

The service

Chandeliers are complex. We know how to handle them.

A chandelier isn't just a light fixture — it's often the centerpiece of a room. Whether it's a grand multi-arm piece with dozens of crystals or a simpler vintage fixture that's stopped working, we have the experience to bring it back to full operation.

With 25+ years in the trade and UL certification, we handle chandeliers of every size and style — assessing each one carefully before recommending any repair.

All sizes welcome

From small dining room fixtures to large multi-tiered chandeliers — bring it in and we'll assess it.

Free estimate

Full cost breakdown before any work begins. No commitment required.

Head technician consult

Our head technician personally evaluates every chandelier and calls you to discuss the repair in detail.

What's covered

What we inspect, repair & replace.

01

Full rewiring

Complete chandelier rewiring — all arms, canopy, and main feed — done safely and to current electrical code.

02

Socket replacement

Individual arm sockets inspected and replaced as needed, matching original style where possible.

03

Crystal & shade replacement

Missing or damaged crystals and shades sourced and replaced to restore the fixture's original look.

04

Canopy & chain inspection

Mounting canopy, chain, and support hardware checked for safety and integrity.

05

Arm & frame repair

Loose, bent, or broken arms tightened or repaired to restore structural stability.

06

Safety inspection

Every chandelier is fully tested before pickup — we won't release a fixture we wouldn't hang in our own home.

How it works

From drop-off to pickup
in 4 simple steps.

1

Call to schedule

Give us a call to arrange your drop-off. For larger chandeliers, let us know in advance so we can prepare.

2

Drop off your chandelier

Bring the fixture to our shop. If it's already been taken down, bring all parts including chain, canopy, and any loose crystals.

3

We assess & call you

Our head technician goes through the fixture arm by arm and calls you personally to walk through the findings, options, and cost — before any work begins.

4

Pick up your restored chandelier

Most repairs are completed within 2 days. We'll call when it's ready and make sure everything is carefully wrapped for transport.
