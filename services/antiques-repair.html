Antiques & Vintage Repair

The service

Old fixtures deserve more than a replacement.

Antique and vintage lamps are often irreplaceable — whether it's a family heirloom, a flea market find, or a piece you've had for decades. With 25+ years of experience and UL certification, we know how to work with older fixtures without compromising what makes them special.

We take care to preserve original finishes, hardware, and materials wherever possible, only replacing what's necessary for safe operation.

Preservation first

We assess every piece carefully and only replace what needs replacing — original parts are kept wherever safe to do so.

Free estimate

We'll examine your fixture and give you a full breakdown before touching anything. No commitment required.

Personal consultation

Our head technician calls you directly to discuss findings and options — you're never left guessing.

What's covered

What we restore & repair.

01

Antique rewiring

Full cord and socket replacement using period-appropriate cloth-covered wire where desired, routed to preserve the original look.

02

Vintage socket restoration

Original sockets repaired or replaced with matching hardware to maintain the fixture's authentic appearance.

03

Hardware assessment

Lamp bodies, harps, finials, and fittings inspected and tightened or sourced as needed.

04

Safety modernization

Updating wiring and components to meet current safety standards without altering the fixture's original aesthetic.

05

Heirloom lamps

Special care taken with sentimental pieces — we treat every antique as if it were our own.

06

Safety inspection

Every restored fixture is tested and inspected before pickup to ensure it's safe and fully functional.

How it works

From drop-off to pickup
in 4 simple steps.

1

Call to schedule

Give us a call and we'll set a convenient drop-off time for your piece.

2

Drop off your fixture

Bring your antique or vintage lamp to our shop. Handle it as carefully as you always have — we'll take it from there.

3

We assess & call you

Our head technician carefully evaluates the piece and calls you personally to walk through what's needed, what can be preserved, and what it will cost.

4

Pick up your restored fixture

Most restorations are completed within 2 days. We'll call when it's ready.
