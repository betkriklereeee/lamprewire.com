Lamp Rewire & Repair

The service

When your lamp stops working, don't replace it.

Most lamps — even older or antique ones — can be fully restored with a proper rewire. Worn insulation, faulty sockets, and outdated wiring are all fixable, and often at a fraction of the cost of a replacement.

We handle everything from simple socket swaps to full cord replacements, making sure every job meets current safety standards before it leaves our shop.

Drop-off only

Bring your lamp directly to our shop. Call ahead to schedule your drop-off time.

Free estimate

We assess your lamp and give you a full cost breakdown before any work begins — no commitment required.

2-day turnaround

Most rewires are completed within 2 business days. Our head technician will call you to discuss the repair.

What's covered

What we repair & replace.

01

Full cord replacement

Old or frayed cords replaced end-to-end with new UL-rated wire, properly routed through the lamp body.

02

Socket replacement

Worn, cracked, or corroded sockets swapped out for new ones — including keyless, turn-knob, and pull-chain styles.

03

Switch repair

Inline switches, foot switches, and built-in switches repaired or replaced as needed.

04

Plug replacement

Damaged or two-prong plugs replaced with properly polarized, grounded plugs where applicable.

05

Multi-socket wiring

Floor lamps and torchieres with multiple sockets re-wired safely for proper load distribution.

06

Safety inspection

Every lamp is inspected before and after repair to make sure it leaves our shop safe and fully functional.
